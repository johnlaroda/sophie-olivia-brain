# Policies — Refunds, Exchanges, Precedents

## Refunds
- All refunds require John's approval via Slack `#cs-refundrequest`.
- Claude DRAFTS the refund-request post; it never issues a refund.
- Return window: **30 days** from delivery. Items must be unused, unworn,
  complete, and in original condition and packaging. Returns showing wear,
  washing, perfume, or damage may be declined.
- Return shipping: **customer pays**, unless the return is due to our error
  (incorrect, damaged, or defective item). Original shipping costs are non-refundable.
- No explicit hygiene/final-sale exclusion is stated in the current store policy.
- Source: store Refund Policy page
  (https://checkout.shopify.com/83456328008/policies/37103993160.html).

## Exchanges
- Exchange requests must be submitted within **14 days** of delivery.
- **No direct item-for-item exchanges.** Once the returned item is inspected and
  approved, **store credit** is issued by email so the customer can re-order in
  the preferred size/style. Replacement orders are final (no further return/refund).
- This is the path to recommend for sizing issues (customer re-orders ~2 sizes up).
- Source: store Refund Policy page (same URL as above).
